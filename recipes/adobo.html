Classic Filipino Style Chicken Adobo

Chicken Adobo is an authentic Filipino dish and is one of the mostly recognized Filipino foods.

It's characterized by its savory, tangy, and slightly sweet flavor profile, achieved through a marinade and braising process using vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Whole peppercorns
  • Bayleaves
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Brown Sugar

How to cook Chicken Adobo

  1. Saute garlic and onion until the garlic turns light brown and the onion softens.
  2. Add your preferred part of chicken Cook until the chicken turns light brown.
  3. Pour water, soy sauce, and vinegar. Let boil.
  4. Add whole peppercorn, and dried bay leaves. Stir. Cover the pot and cook in medium heat for 15 minutes.
  5. Add brown sugar and season with salt.
  6. Transfer to a serving bowl. Serve. Share and enjoy!
