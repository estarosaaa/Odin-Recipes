Filipino Style Spaghetti

Filipino spaghetti (also known as sweet spaghetti) is a Filipino adaptation of Italian spaghetti with Bolognese sauce.

It has a distinctively sweet sauce, usually made from tomato sauce sweetened with brown sugar, banana ketchup, or condensed milk.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon Butter
  • 1/2 Cup onion finely chopped
  • 1/2 Cup carrot finely chopped
  • 1/2 Cup celery finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon Garlic finely chopped
  • 250 Grams Beef use ground round
  • 250 Grams pork use ground pork
  • 7 pieces Red hotdogs diced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce use sweet-style sauce
  • 1/2 Cup water
  • 1 piece Knorr Chicken Cube
  • Salt
  • 350 Grams spaghetti noodles cooked according to package directions
  • 1/2 Cup quick- melting cheese grated

Here's how to cook it

  1. In a medium skillet, heat oil and melt butter. Add onions, carrots, and celery; cook until vegetables are tender. Add garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  2. Add ground round, ground pork, and bay leaves. Brown the meat for about 10 minutes. Halfway through cooking, add the red hotdogs; mix well. Remove the excess oil.
  3. Pour in tomato sauce, Knorr Chicken Cubes, and water or stock; mix well. Let meat mixture simmer over low heat for 8 to 10 minutes. Season with salt, and pepper. Continue to cook for 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. When ready to serve, toss pasta with meat sauce until well combined. Top with grated cheese or serve cheese on the side.
